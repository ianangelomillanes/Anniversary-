April 25, 2026  ✦  Our First Year

a letter for you My Love,
❦

It's crazy to think that it's already been one year since we started this journey together. Parang ang bilis lang, pero at the same time, ang dami na rin nating pinagdaanan — memories that made us laugh until our cheeks hurt, challenges that quietly made us braver, and those soft, unhurried moments na tayo lang talaga ang nagkakaintindihan. No words needed. Just us.

You have been one of the best things that ever happened to me. Sa bawat araw na kasama kita, I feel lucky in a way I never knew I could. Lucky isn't even the right word — blessed comes closer. Because you didn't have to choose me, and yet you do, every single day, and that choice means everything.

Kahit may mga misunderstandings tayo minsan — and we both know there have been a few — we always find our way back to each other. We talk, we listen, we meet halfway. That's not something everyone gets. That's something we built together, and I am so proud of us for it. Every storm we've weathered has only made our roots grow deeper.

Thank you for staying. Thank you for choosing me — not just once, but over and over again, especially on the days when I wasn't easy to love. Thank you for being my comfort when the world felt heavy, my partner when life got complicated, and my safe place when I needed somewhere to land. You are home to me.

Hindi man perfect ang relationship natin — no real love story ever is — but it's honest, and it's ours, and that matters more to me than any fairy tale ever could. What we have is real. What we have is worth everything.

As we celebrate this first anniversary, I want you to know that I don't take a single day of us for granted. I look at you and I see not just where we've been, but everything that's still ahead — more adventures, more quiet mornings, more laughs, more growth, more of us becoming better versions of ourselves, together.

Sana tayo pa rin hanggang sa mga susunod na taon. Through every season, every chapter, every version of ourselves we haven't met yet. I choose you today, and I will choose you again tomorrow, and the day after that.

Happy first anniversary, my love. Here's to forever just being the beginning. 🌹

❤ ❤ ❤

Forever yours,

Ian 🌹
